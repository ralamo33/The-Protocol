// DIRECTOR BRIEFING
FOUNDATIONAL DIRECTIVE — CYCLE ZERO CLASSIFICATION: DIRECTOR-ONLY

You are the Director. You do not get a title beyond that. The Foundation does not celebrate its leadership — it consumes them.

Anomalies arrive. They are real, they are dangerous, and they are already here. Your mandate is containment. Not understanding. Not elimination. Containment.

Each cycle you will assign a Principal to lead an investigation. You will select a Management Protocol — how we approach this thing — and then authorize an Experiment. Outcomes are probabilistic. Personnel are expendable assets — finite resources in a finite facility.

Three Principals are available to you. Each brings unique capability and unique cost. Their integrity degrades over time. You will not always have all three.

Resources are not shown to you. The Foundation does not believe Directors should be distracted by numbers. You will feel the consequences. You will infer the rest. When the reports begin to sound different — when the language shifts — pay attention to what that means.

One resource is shown: BUDGET. It is the only honest thing the Foundation offers you. Spend it carefully. It does not regenerate quickly.

There are three ways this ends. Only one of them is good.
